 Wait.. but why?

It all starts with the Private Key, which is nothing but a random number within the range of 1 to 2256 that looks like this when represented as a string of hexadecimal characters: 5222a17e1db9763fa397b97d8da35eacd678fa6f8de148c511618a06f453d0d6. From it, it's very easy to generate a Public Key (somethig like this e6f7b7af1dcaf9f1d4100ffc68fd2bb355e7ec9b3893a04773bcd2804f641346) in such a mathematical way that it is impossible to guess the Private Key from it (it's a one way function). However, it's not easy to obtain a Publick Key with a predefined pattern, such as let's say, starting with four zeros (0000d82a3bfd...). The only way is trial and error, which means to generate a Private Key, obtain the corresponding Public Key and see how many zeros does it have!. The more strict the pattern is (ie: more zeros) the more difficult becomes to obtain it (to mine it) and that too shows to the world that you've worked hard to obtain your Public Key!

Every Nostr user is represented by a Public Key. There is no cost at all in obtaining a genereic Publick Key. That means bots 🤖 and spammers 🧛 can create unlimited accounts at no cost (either computationally or economically). A way to shield against them is to apply an entrance toll: No Public Key with less than a given number of zeros will be accepted. You, as a user, will have to mine your Public Key only once, but when bots are detected and banned, they will have to work again to generate a new valid Publick Key. It's a way to discourage bad behaviour.

You must understand that it is a process of exploration, not generation. The haystack in which we look for the needle is as big as the universe 🌌, and very few needles meet the criteria (and chances get even smaller the more zeros you add to your desired key 🔑 ). It can take days to find it! There are other tools that work faster because they talk directly to the hardware. pubpow.com uses in-browser javascript. You just have to leave it running.

You don't loose anything, because it is a random search! The search simply ceases. Come back at any other moment and resume it. At pubpow.com we are working on a Chrome extension that allows you to close this tab but still continue mining (as long as you have the browser open)

Since this is a mathematical universe 🌌, you don't need to be plugged to the internet, even though you are mining with your browser. Crazy, uh? What you are mining is a private key associated with a special public key. The private key should only be known by you and no one else. We don't collect any data whatsoever. Don't believe me? Just turn off your wifi and keep mining until you find your key 🔑 and don't ever come back here 🙋‍!.
